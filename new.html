Trip Hightlights

Mall Road Shimla

Mall Road in Shimla is the heart of the city's shopping, dining, and sightseeing experience. Lined with colonial-era buildings, vibrant shops, and cozy cafes, it offers a perfect blend of history and modernity. Visitors can enjoy leisurely walks, shop for Himachali handicrafts, and savor delicious local and international cuisines. With popular attractions like Scandal Point, Kali Bari Temple, and Gaiety Theatre nearby, Mall Road is a must-visit destination for tourists seeking the true charm of Shimla.

Jakhoo Temple Shimla

Jakhoo Temple in Shimla is a renowned ancient temple dedicated to Lord Hanuman, situated on the highest peak of Shimla at Jakhu Hill. It holds great mythological significance, believed to be the spot where Hanuman rested while on his mission to find the Sanjeevani herb during the Ramayana era. The temple is famous for its massive 108-feet tall Hanuman statue, one of the tallest in the world, overlooking the beautiful Shivalik ranges. Surrounded by dense deodar forests, Jakhoo Temple offers a serene spiritual experience along with panoramic views of Shimla city, making it a must-visit destination on your Shimla Manali honeymoon travel package.

Solang Valley Manali

Solang Valley in Manali is a paradise for adventure lovers and nature enthusiasts. Known for its stunning snow-capped mountains and lush greenery, it offers thrilling activities like skiing, snowboarding, paragliding, zorbing, and ATV rides. Whether visiting in winter for snow sports or summer for trekking and camping, Solang Valley promises unforgettable experiences and breathtaking landscapes. It is a must-visit spot on your Shimla Manali honeymoon travel package for both adventure and serene natural beauty.

Vashisht Hot Springs

Vashisht Hot Spring in Manali is a natural thermal spring located in the tranquil village of Vashisht, just 3 km from Manali town. Renowned for its medicinal properties, the sulfur-rich hot water is believed to cure various skin diseases and ease joint pains. This sacred site is home to the ancient Vashisht Temple dedicated to the sage Vashisht, offering visitors both spiritual solace and a rejuvenating bath experience. With separate bathing areas for men and women, the hot springs provide a unique blend of wellness, heritage, and scenic beauty, making it a must-visit destination on your Shimla Manali honeymoon travel package.

Hadimba Temple Manali

Hadimba Temple in Manali is a historic and architecturally unique temple dedicated to Hidimba Devi, the wife of Bhima from the Indian epic Mahabharata. Built in 1553 by Maharaja Bahadur Singh, this temple is renowned for its distinctive pagoda-style architecture with intricately carved wooden doors and a multi-tiered timber roof. Nestled amidst a tranquil cedar forest, the temple offers a serene spiritual ambiance coupled with stunning natural beauty. A must-visit on your Shimla Manali honeymoon travel package, Hadimba Temple is a blend of cultural heritage, mythology, and Himalayan charm.
